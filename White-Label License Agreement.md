## 4. FEES AND PAYMENT TERMS

### 4.1 Monthly License Fee
The Licensee agrees to pay a flat monthly license fee of:

**$600.00 USD per month**

This fee covers platform access, hosting infrastructure, and ongoing maintenance as described in Section 2.3.

### 4.2 Consultation Commission
In addition to the monthly license fee, the Provider earns a commission on each completed patient consultation processed through the Platform:

| Monthly Consultation Volume | Commission Rate |
|-----------------------------|-----------------|
| 1 – 200 consultations       | 5% per visit    |
| 201+ consultations          | 3% per visit    |

A "completed consultation" is defined as a patient submission successfully recorded in the Platform database.

**Example at $49.99 per visit:**
- 100 visits/month = $249.95 commission
- 250 visits/month = $499.90 (first 200) + $74.99 (50 above 200) = $574.89 commission

### 4.3 Payment Schedule
- Monthly license fee is due on the **1st of each month**
- Commission is calculated and invoiced on the **last day of each month** based on verified submission counts from the Admin Dashboard
